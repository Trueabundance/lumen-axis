Privacy Policy for LumenAxis

Your privacy is important to us. This policy applies to all apps by LumenAxis, including LumenDose and LumenFuel.

1. Information We Collect

We only ask for personal information when we truly need it to provide a service to you. The only personal data we collect is an anonymous user ID provided by Firebase Authentication to store your log data securely for each app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected anonymous data to store your log history in our database (Firebase Firestore), allowing you to track your consumption over time within the respective app. This data is tied to your anonymous user ID and is not shared.
